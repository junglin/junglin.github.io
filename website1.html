    Girls Who Code is a seven week immersion program that empowers women through mentoring and coding. At the end of the program, the girls use all of the skills that they learned throughout the seven week period to work on a project.

    The Girls Who Code location that I attended was in the San Francisco Twitter Headquarters. At the end of the program, we learned how to use and create softwares in: Scratch, Python, HTML, CSS, Javascript, Robotics.
